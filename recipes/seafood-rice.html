Seafood Rice

A beautiful, quick and easy to make Seafood Rice recipe.
For when you want a simple yet delicious meal after a hard day at work

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1.2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of pepper
  • tablespoon of cajun
  • tablespoon of smoked paprika
  • tomato paste
  • Last but not least - the seafood. Fresh or frozen - doesn't metter

Preparation

  1. Wash the rice until water becomes clear. Afterwards put them aside to dry.
  2. Chop tomato in big chunks
  3. Dice the onion into smaller pieces
  4. Sweatout the onion in the pan and add choped tomatoes
  5. Add tomato paste and give it a good stir
  6. Add rice to the pan and mix it well to fully incorporate the rice with the spices
  7. Add the chicken broth
  8. Wait for water to boild and then cover the pan and turn the heat to medium-low
  9. After 12 minutes add the frozen or cooked seafood and cover the pan again
  10. Just three more minutes to wait
  11. Voila! You can remove the cover now and enjoy your beutifuly prepared Seafood Rice!
